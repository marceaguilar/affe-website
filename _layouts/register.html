Submission Form

To set up the submission form:

  1. Create a Google Form for submissions
  2. Click "Send" → Click the "<>" (embed) icon
  3. Copy the iframe src URL
  4. Edit pages/register.md and add google_form_url: "YOUR_URL" to the front matter

Quick tip: The Google Form iframe will automatically handle submissions and send data to your Google Sheet.
