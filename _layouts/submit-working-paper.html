Submit Your Working Paper

Share your research with the AFFE community. Submit your working paper through our submission form below. Our team will review all submissions and publish accepted papers on our Working Papers page.

Submit Your Paper

What You'll Need to Submit:

  • Paper Title - The full title of your working paper
  • Author(s) - Full name(s) of all authors
  • University/Affiliation - Institutional affiliation(s) for each author
  • PDF File - Complete working paper as a PDF document
  • Abstract - Brief summary of your paper (optional but recommended)
