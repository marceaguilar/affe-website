        <h3 style="color: #5A4A3A; font-size: 1.2em; margin-bottom: 1rem;">Call for Papers</h3>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 1rem; line-height: 1.7;">We welcome manuscripts using field experiments to address important questions in accounting, economics, and finance. Consideration will be given to studies that:</p>
        <ol style="padding-left: 1.5rem; line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 0;">

          <li>Use behavioral interventions to reduce inefficiencies in the market;</li>
          <li>Leverage field experiments to test theoretical predictions or calibrate economic models.</li>
        </ol>
